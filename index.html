Assessment Reality Check

At 93kg, your joints are under significant mechanical load, exacerbated by the kinetic compensations from your right BKA. Your left leg is taking the brunt of the force, and your left shoulder is acting as a counterweight.

The Mission: Strip body fat to reduce joint load, strengthen the posterior chain to fix the lateral lean, and eliminate exercises that cause unnecessary impact. Nutrition will drive the weight loss; this protocol builds the engine.

The Iron Rules

Violate these, and the compensations will return.

  • Chest-Supported Only: Because your lower body foundation is asymmetrical, standing overhead presses or bent-over rows will force your lower back to twist. Always use a bench or machine to support your chest/back during upper body movements.
  • The 2:1 Pull Ratio: For every pushing set (chest/front delts), you must do TWO pulling sets (back/rear delts). This pulls your shoulders back and fights the forward slump.
  • Trap Stop Protocol: If you are doing lateral raises or rows and feel your neck/upper traps doing the work instead of the target muscle, drop the weight immediately. Your left trap is already overworked.
  • Diet Dictates Gravity: At 93kg, every step sends massive force through your left foot and right socket. You cannot out-train a bad diet. Caloric deficit is a biomechanical requirement, not just a cosmetic one.
